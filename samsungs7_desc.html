Description

Samsung Galaxy S7 is the perfect balance between form and technology that you can not help it. The rounded edges and care given to the design starting with harmonious lines up to a thickness size make it even more convenient and enjoyable to use
The 5.1-inch Quad HD display has a well density of 577ppi which offers a visual quality that goes beyond the imaginable. With the functionality Always On the display information without being totally active.
It is an intelligent function that automatically turns off when you put the phone in your pocket or bag, when the display is face down or when the battery is low. Galaxy S7 is also resistant to water and dust allowing you to play on the edge of a swimming pool, to publish selfie snapped at the beach or to call a taxi in the rain quietly.
The new custom CPU, the powerful GPU and 4GB of RAM form a winning combination for the best use of all the functionality offered by smartphones. Even the most graphically advanced games will run smoothly and without overheating. Galaxy S7 is the first smartphone to support Vulkan API for you to enjoy a unique gaming experience.
Thanks to the support of microSD card you can expand the memory capacity according to your needs so as to avoid delete your favorite content. The focal-length lens aperture F1.7 and innovative features of the rear camera 12 megapixel capture more light and produce more vivid photos. All pixels on the image sensor are provided with two photodiodes instead of one. This means that the Dual Pixel Sensor can focus with the same speed and precision of the human eye.
The 5 megapixel front camera instead owns the panoramic selfies function that lets you take a group selfie up to 120? to include all your friends in the shot. Galaxy S7 also supports fast and has a much more powerful battery charger that allows you to watch your videos and listen to your favorite music for hours. Full support for 4G + Plus networks!
